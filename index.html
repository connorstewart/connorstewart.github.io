About Us

Kevin Ryan, a longtime resident of Huntington is the founder/owner of KMR Painting & Decorating. He has been in business as a painting contractor since 1983. With the experience he has acquired over 32 years, along with his boundless dedication to providing excellence with integrity, Kevin Ryan is considered the premier painter of the community.
